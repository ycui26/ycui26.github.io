I am a final year Ph.D. student in Department of Computer Science, City University of Hong Kong (CityU), advised by Prof. [Man Hon Cheung](https://www.cs.cityu.edu.hk/~mhcheung/index.htm). I was a visiting student at Northwestern University from Sept 2025 to Feb 2026, hosted by Prof. [Randall Berry](https://www.mccormick.northwestern.edu/research-faculty/directory/profiles/berry-randy.html) and Prof. [Ermin Wei](https://www.mccormick.northwestern.edu/research-faculty/directory/profiles/wei-ermin.html). From Sept 2021 to May 2022, I was a research assistant in Department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science, University of Tennessee, Knoxville (UTK), advised by Prof. [Jian Liu](https://web.eecs.utk.edu/~jliu/). I received my B.Eng. degree in Computer Science and Technology (with a double major in Finance) from University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC) in June 2021. 


Research Interests
------
My research focuses on the intersection of artificial intelligence, data privacy, and network economics.
- **Trustworthy ML**: Machine Unlearning, Federated Learning, Adversarial Attack and Defense
- **Economics and Optimization**: Incentive and Pricing Mechanism, Game Theory, Convex Optimization, Online Optimization
